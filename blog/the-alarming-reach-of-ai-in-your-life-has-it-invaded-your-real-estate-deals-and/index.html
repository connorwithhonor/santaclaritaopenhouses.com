Privacy Permissions: The Hidden Danger Lurking in AI

This is where it gets sarcastic: Sure, give AI your credit card and say, “Book Italy”—what could go wrong? God knows it’d buy tickets to everywhere, based on your data.

Seriously, permissions are crucial. In real estate, granting MLS access once meant confidential data; now, scrapers exploit it. AI amps this—access your phone, and it trains on texts, photos, everything.

Unless you privatize (often impossible without denying access), data shares. My suggestion: Read fine print. How deep does access go? Every email ever? Contacts’ info?
