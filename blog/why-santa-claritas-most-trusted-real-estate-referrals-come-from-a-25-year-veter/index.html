The Problem With How Most People Find Real Estate Agents in Santa Clarita

Let’s be honest: finding a real estate agent shouldn’t feel like a gamble, but for most people in Santa Clarita Valley, that’s exactly what it is.

You go to Zillow. You click “Find an Agent.” The algorithm spits out three names based on… what exactly? Who paid the most for your contact information? Who responded fastest to the last inquiry? Who has the best SEO game?

Here’s what those platforms don’t tell you:

They don’t vet for integrity. They don’t verify track records. They don’t know if that agent will ghost you when the deal gets complicated. And they certainly don’t care if you’re matched with someone who has your best interests at heart—or just wants to close fast and move on.

I spent 25 years selling real estate in Santa Clarita and 20 years as an LAPD officer. I’ve seen every type of agent—the ones who work their tails off for clients, and the ones who view people as transaction numbers. I’ve watched deals fall apart because an agent didn’t communicate. I’ve seen buyers overpay because their agent didn’t negotiate. I’ve seen sellers leave tens of thousands on the table because their agent wanted a quick close instead of fighting for top dollar.

That’s why I went referral-only.

Not because I couldn’t compete. Not because I was tired of selling. But because I realized something: I’m better at connecting people with the right professionals than I am at being one more agent fighting for listings.

What People Ask Me About Santa Clarita Real Estate Referrals

Over the past few years, I’ve had hundreds of conversations with buyers, sellers, and agents about the referral process. Here are the most common questions:

“How is a referral agent different from a traditional agent?”

A traditional agent represents you directly in a transaction. They show you homes, write offers, negotiate on your behalf, and handle the closing process.

A referral agent (like me) connects you with the best traditional agent for your specific situation, based on personal relationships and proven performance.

Think of it this way: I’m like a matchmaker. I know both sides—I know you (after our conversation) and I know the agents in my network (after 25 years of working with them). I make introductions that are more likely to result in great outcomes because they’re based on fit, not algorithms.

”Do I pay extra for a referral?”

Nope. The agent I refer you to pays me a referral fee from their commission—you don’t pay anything extra. The commission you’d pay working directly with an agent is the same as the commission you’d pay working with an agent I refer.

The difference? You get the benefit of my 25 years of experience in selecting the right professional for you, at no additional cost.
