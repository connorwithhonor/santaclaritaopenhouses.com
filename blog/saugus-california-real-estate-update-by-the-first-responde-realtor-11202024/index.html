Where is Saugus, CA?

Saugus, part of the beautiful Santa Clarita Valley, is nestled between the 14 Freeway and the 5 Freeway, creating a scenic “Y” shape. Known for top-rated schools, family-friendly neighborhoods, beautiful parks, and walking paths, Saugus is perfect for everyone—from families to singles looking for a vibrant community to call home.
