A Closer Look at Three-Bedroom Detached Homes in Santa Clarita

One of the most in-demand categories in Santa Clarita is the three-bedroom detached single-family home. Here’s a breakdown of the current three-bedroom listings across some of the most sought-after neighborhoods:

Valencia:

Valencia is known for its family-friendly neighborhoods and access to top-rated schools. Right now, there are several three-bedroom homes for sale, including one in the San Marino neighborhood listed at $949,999 and another in Chelsea for $995,000. Multiple listings in neighborhoods like these indicate that buyers may have room to negotiate.

Saugus:

Saugus offers a variety of three-bedroom homes. For instance, there’s one listed for $699,000 in the Lookout Point neighborhood, and another in Shadow Ridge for $800,000. This area provides excellent options for buyers looking for a balance of affordability and quality.

Canyon Country:

Canyon Country is a popular choice for buyers looking for a bit more space. Homes here start in the mid $700,000s, such as a three-bedroom home in the North Oaks neighborhood, listed at $775,000. If you want land and privacy, this is the area to explore.

Newhall:

In Newhall, the inventory is more limited, but quality is high. A custom home in Downtown Newhall is listed at $799,999. Buyers looking in this area need to act fast as properties tend to sell quickly.

Stevenson Ranch:

Known for its luxury market, Stevenson Ranch has a three-bedroom home listed at $1.195 million in the Summits/Chelsea neighborhood. This area offers high-end homes with premium features and stunning views, perfect for buyers looking for upscale living.

Castaic:

Castaic is ideal for buyers seeking larger lots and a more suburban feel. For example, there’s a three-bedroom home on Meadowgrass Drive listed at $760,000. Castaic is known for its quieter atmosphere and spacious properties, making it a great choice for families.
