Coming Soon listings entering the Santa Clarita home market

Connor “with Honor” MacIvor - October 27, 2024** 0 Comments | Add Comment Welcome to the Santa Clarita Valley, a community that’s buzzing with exciting new properties soon to hit the market! From classic single-story homes to sprawling estates with unique features, there’s something here for everyone. In the spirit of keeping things fresh and building anticipation, we’re sharing an insider look at 21 upcoming homes. Whether you’re searching for space, style, or neighborhood charm, these teasers will give you a taste of what’s coming soon.

1. Saugus Emblem Tract Pool Home

Located on Elias Ave in Saugus, this 4-bedroom, 2-bath home brings classic California living to the heart of Santa Clarita Valley. With a spacious yard, private pool, and updates throughout, it’s the perfect match for anyone dreaming of that relaxed poolside lifestyle. Plenty of parking, no HOA, and proximity to award-winning schools make this a prime spot.

2. Alta Knoll Custom Estate

This Bouquet Canyon gem is rare, and it won’t be available for long. Set on a private road with exclusive views, this 6-bedroom home on Alta Knoll Dr. includes a main house and a two-bedroom additional structure perfect for multi-generational living. With a circular driveway, expansive grounds, and room for all your outdoor toys, this single-story estate has everything you could desire.

3. Canyon Country Updated View Home

Nestled on Merryhill St, this 4-bedroom, 2-bath home offers breathtaking views with a quiet suburban feel. Inside, enjoy an open floor plan and well-lit spaces, making it ideal for family gatherings or serene relaxation. The lack of HOA fees makes it an even more attractive opportunity for those looking for affordable luxury.

4. Executive Home in Canyon Crest

For those seeking both luxury and tranquility, this 4-bedroom home on Hawks Ridge Dr. offers a unique layout with an oversized yard. It has everything from a private loft and bonus room to stunning hilltop views. With three bathrooms, an open floor plan, and a welcoming atmosphere, it’s the perfect retreat in the hills.

5. Tres Robles Townhome Treasure

Discover a well-designed 3-bedroom, 3-bath end-unit townhome on River Cir, featuring direct garage access and spacious rooms. A short distance from the freeway and shopping, it’s an ideal choice for those seeking convenience. Inside, recent upgrades and a classic stone fireplace create a cozy space with an added touch of elegance.

6. Valencia’s New Gem in Five Points

Set within the prestigious Five Points Valencia community, this modern 3-bedroom condo on Evening Sky Pl. offers resort-style amenities like pools, cabanas, and even a community garden. The open-plan living and dining areas with quartz countertops in the kitchen make it both beautiful and functional. Plus, solar panels make it energy efficient.

7. Hillcrest’s Corner Lot Delight

Located on Iris Pl in Castaic, this 3-bedroom home sits on a huge corner lot within a gated community. Fresh paint, a new balcony deck off the primary bedroom, and well-maintained yards create an inviting atmosphere. Low HOA fees and access to community amenities make this a perfect choice for families.

8. Entertainer’s Dream in Hasley Hills

This beautifully updated home on Cambridge Ave offers an entertainer’s dream layout with a large open kitchen flowing into a private backyard. The spacious bedrooms and upgraded features make it the perfect family home, ideal for casual evenings or grand gatherings.

9. Northbridge Townhome with Flair

Located in the desirable Northbridge area, this 3-bedroom townhome on Cheyenne Dr. combines townhome convenience with the feel of a single-family home. The open-plan layout and cozy fireplace make it a great place to gather, while HOA amenities like pools and playgrounds add to the family-friendly vibe.

10. Peaceful Retreat in Newhall’s Milestone Tract

This spacious 4-bedroom home on Lindale Pl offers over 2,000 square feet of elegance and privacy. Plantation shutters, stylish flooring, and a private community entrance create a peaceful retreat close to shopping and freeway access.

11. Castaic Oaks Detached Home

This 4-bedroom detached condo on Nichols Ln in Castaic offers both privacy and convenience, with a pool, spa, and solar panels. The open-plan layout downstairs is perfect for entertaining, while the decorative wrought iron railing adds an elegant touch.

12. Stevenson Ranch Pool Home

Located in the desirable Spyglass neighborhood, this 4-bedroom home on Chisom Ln offers an open concept living space with vaulted ceilings and a beautiful backyard pool area. The upgraded primary suite and proximity to award-winning schools make it an ideal choice for families looking to settle in the sought-after Stevenson Ranch area.

13. Valencia’s Charming Condo

For those looking to downsize without sacrificing style, this 2-bedroom condo on Lyons Ave offers a well-planned layout and ample closet space. Located in a community with excellent amenities, it’s close to shopping and dining.

14. Single-Story Las Ventanas Townhome

Rare and inviting, this single-story townhome on McBean Ave has vaulted ceilings and an attached 2-car garage. Inside, the kitchen features original wood cabinets, while the living area boasts a private patio.

15. Cozy Sunrise Tract Home

Located on Lupita Dr., this 3-bedroom Valencia home offers remodeled spaces, a private yard, and walkable access to shopping and schools. A nearby community pool and spa make it ideal for those seeking a convenient lifestyle with ample recreational options.

16. Valencia Glen Potential

On Avenida Jolita, this 3-bedroom home is waiting for the right buyer to make it their own. With an enclosed patio, in-ground jacuzzi, and easy access to top-rated schools, it’s a fantastic opportunity for someone looking to add their personal touch.

17. Valencia Summit Estate

This 4-bedroom estate on Turnstone Ct is a standout with its end-of-cul-de-sac location. Inside, upgraded features and luxurious touches throughout the home offer a tranquil escape. Outdoor spaces offer picturesque views, multiple patios, and access to community amenities.

18. Val Verde Single Story

On Verdale Ave in Castaic’s Val Verde area, this recently remodeled single-story home includes a bonus room with a separate entrance. With stylish kitchen upgrades, double-pane windows, and a private backyard, this home is move-in ready.

19. Fixer Upper with Big Potential on Paradise Rd

This ranch-style home on 2.5 acres is brimming with possibilities for those with a creative vision. Featuring 3 bedrooms and access to public water, electricity, and septic, it’s ideal for those interested in rural living or creating a unique project.

20. Val Verde’s Expansive Retreat

This 3-bedroom home on Paradise Rd offers a vast lot and serene views. With zoning for light agricultural projects and animal keeping, it’s perfect for aspiring farmers or those seeking a peaceful escape from city life.

21. Valencia’s Hidden Gem on Turnstone Ct

This exquisite 4-bedroom home at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac in the Chelsea Collection offers a private oasis with upgraded interiors. Multiple outdoor areas provide space for entertaining, and access to community amenities ensures a vibrant lifestyle.

Each of these homes offers its own unique style and appeal, making the Santa Clarita Valley a dynamic place to call home. Keep an eye out as these “coming soon” properties start to hit the market. Whether you’re a first-time buyer or a seasoned investor, Santa Clarita has something special just for you. For more information or to schedule a viewing, don’t hesitate to reach out – these homes won’t stay available for long!
